How Catalyst Impacted the Region in 2020

COVID-19 turned our world upside down. Supply chains were disrupted. Jobs were lost. But we still managed to rise to the challenge and overcome many obstacles during a turbulent year. From securing grants for local manufacturers impacted by the pandemic, to creating new training opportunities to upskill new and incumbent workers, we are proud of all that we accomplished and humbled to share these accomplishments with you through our annual report.
